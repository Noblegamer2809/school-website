sports

Vidyashilp has achieved several laurels in Sports over the years. Our teams take part in around 40 - 50 Inter-School tournaments every academic year. Inter-School competitions are conducted in the school in almost all games. In addition, an annual Sports Day is hosted at school which resembles a ‘Mini Olympics’.
